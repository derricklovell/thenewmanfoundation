Who We Are

Tyrone L. & Felicia A. Newman, Founders.
The Newman Foundation is a family-based foundation with a heart to help the community God planted them in. The Newman Foundation organized in 2004 and incorporated in 2010. Our vision is to serve our most vulnerable citizens and aid in the quality of life for all. It is our responsibility to take a bold stand and become part of the solution in the rebuilding of our communities. Developing families and strengthen relationships one at a time is our mission. This vision is to be a beacon of light in a dark world. The Foundation’s vision includes two social areas of need: Food Relief and Community Partnership.

Programs We Offer

Felicia Newman's Food Pantry
The Newman Foundation operates The Felicia Newman’s Food Pantries. We have two locations with one mission; We serve at 20647 Midway Road, Terry MS 39170 on the 1st and 3rd Mondays from 10:00am-12:00noon. We serve at 1110 Grand Ave, Jackson MS 39203 on the 3rd Wednesday of each month from 5:00pm-6:30pm. Please bring your photo ID for pickups. Emergency Food Assistance is also available to those in need, there is never a cost to recipients.
Other Programs
The Newman Foundation partners with several agencies and organizations to ensure we are doing our part to support our community. We partner with agencies, corporate America, churches and other organizations that have the common goal. We host Thelma Allen’s Supper and distribute Rufus Newman’s Blessing Bags each month to a local personal care facility. Charlene Newman’s College Support provides monthly stipends to eligible college students. Hester Powell’s Senior Feeding Program offers support strictly to our senior citizens. Did you know that more than half of Mississippi’s Seniors experience regular shortfalls in food? Lastly, through Alfreddie Powell’s African Foreign Mission we support a Christian School in Liberia West Africa that not only teaches both the young and old but also houses the students in need. They also build local churches in the communities that surround the school.
